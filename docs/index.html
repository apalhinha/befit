Exercise Catalog

Workout and exercises are accessible to everyone and can easily be found on the web. Frequently, and specially for those not used to it, ‘what to do’, or ‘how to do’ are challenges. This site tries to fix this.

This project is currently in a very beginner stage. A few examples of planned content are the current list of exercises, a progression of exercises to achieve the floor L-Sit, a collection of variants for the push-up, and moves you can do with a given piece of equipment, say the pull-up bar.
