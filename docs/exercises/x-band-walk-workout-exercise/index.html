X Band Walk. How-to, proper form and tips

2022-11-13

The X-Band Walk is a lateral walk where an elastic band is used to challenge the leg muscles.

Video demonstration

Excerpt from 'X Band Walks' by Forma Gym

How to and step by step instructions:

  1. Step the elastic band with both feet, shoulder-width apart.
  2. Cross the band end points, so that one hand holds the band coming from the other foot, both forming an X.
  3. Walk to the side, moving one leg away from the other, and then bring the other one close.

Hints & tips:

  • Keep a slight bend in the knees while walking.

Benefits:

  • Increase muscular strength on glutes and hip abductors.
  • Stabilizes the ankles, knees and hips.
  • Great warm up exercise.

Counting & gamification:

  • Count the walked distance in meters.

Muscles worked and body parts involved:

Primary: Glutes, Hip Abductor
