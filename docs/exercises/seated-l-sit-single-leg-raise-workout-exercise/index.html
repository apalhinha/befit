Seated L Sit Single Leg Raise. How-to, proper form and tips

2022-11-04

Sit on the floor in a perfect L position and raise one leg at a time keeping it in a straight position.

Video demonstration

Excerpt from 'Seated L Sit Single Leg Lift' by AchieveFitnessBoston

How to and step by step instructions:

  1. Sit down on the floor, legs straight, feet together, hands on the ground hips side.
  2. Raise the other leg keeping it in a straight position.
  3. Lower the leg and raise the other one.

Hints & tips:

  • Keep your back in a straight position, shoulders pressed down and ears away from the shoulders.
  • Breath normally.
  • Keep the leg on the floor as straight as possible.

Benefits:

  • Exercise is a basic L Sit preparation move.
  • Excellent back exercise.

Counting & gamification:

  • Count each time one of the legs is up.
  • Set a goal for a given period, session, week or month and count repetitions until you reach the goal.
