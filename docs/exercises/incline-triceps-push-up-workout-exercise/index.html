Incline Triceps Push Up. How-to, proper form and tips

2022-11-19
Bodyweight

The Incline Triceps Push Up strengthens the triceps in a mid-term between the easier Wall Triceps Push Up and the harder Knee Triceps Push Up.

Exercise is also known as Incline Triceps Extension.

Video demonstration

Excerpt from 'Bodyweight Triceps Extensions' by Andrew Dixon

How to and step by step instructions:

  1. Choose a balcony, horizontal bar or any appliance as high as your shoulders.
  2. Hold such support with both hands, arms bended and elbows towards the floor.
  3. Move your feet away so than the body performs a 45 degrees angle.
  4. This is the starting position.
  5. Extend your arms bringing the body a little more vertical.
  6. Bend your elbows again, keeping them vertical toward the floor, not horizontally.

Hints & tips:

  • The lower your hands, the harder the movement.

Benefits:

  • Strengthen Triceps.

Counting & gamification:

  • Do 3 sets of 7-10 repetitions.
