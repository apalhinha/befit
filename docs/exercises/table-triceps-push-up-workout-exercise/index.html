Table Triceps Push Up. How-to, proper form and tips

2022-11-05
Calisthenics, Strength

Kind of incline push up using nothing but the triceps.

Exercise is also known as Table Triceps Extension.

Video demonstration

Excerpt from 'Bodyweight Table Tricep Extension' by PT On a Plane

How to and step by step instructions:

  1. Hold the table border with both hands in shoulder width.
  2. Extend your arms, engage core, feet far enough to have the legs in a 45 degree inclination. This is the starting position.
  3. Bent your elbows, keep them pointing to the ground without opening them.
  4. Extend both arms again, returning to the starting position.

Hints & tips:

  • Use a higher table for an easier variation.
  • Move your feet closer to the table for an easier variation.

Benefits:

  • Strengthen triceps.

Counting & gamification:

  • As a beginner, aim for 10 to 10 reps.
  • Move to the Knee Triceps Push Up or Triceps Push Up when reaching the 10 to 15 reps.
