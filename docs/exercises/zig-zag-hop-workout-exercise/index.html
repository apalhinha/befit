Zig Zag Hop. How-to, proper form and tips

2022-11-19
Cardio, Plyometrics

Zig Zag Hop is an Plyometric exercise where you jump with your feet together, in diagonal left and right.

Video demonstration

Excerpt from 'Zig Zag Hop' by LundtG

How to and step by step instructions:

  1. Set in the starting point, feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Jump forward, in diagonal to the left, using both legs.
  3. Right after landing, repeat the jump in diagonal to the right.
  4. Repeat until a given distance.

Hints & tips:

  • Jump as high and far as you can, without losing balance.

Benefits:

  • Explosive strength.
  • Jump capacity.

Counting & gamification:

  • Measure and count the covered distance or number of jumps.
  • Set a distance goal for a period, count the accumulated distance amongst all sessions in that period.

Muscles worked and body parts involved:

Primary: Glutes, Quads, Calves

Secondary: Hamstring, Feet
