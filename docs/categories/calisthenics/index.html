Calisthenics - List of exercises

Calisthenics exercises are made with minimal or no equipment at all. They address muscles groups and most of them are bodyweight exercises.

Some exercises are harder than others, although a progression of moves can facilitate the most difficult ones. See for example the Floor L-Sit progression.
